# SportKorner

SportKorner is a premium sports journalism and GIS mapping platform focused on the 2026 World Cup and Arabic football.

## Key Sections
- /articles: Community-driven sports journalism and analysis.
- /news: Breaking global sports news.
- /tournaments: Tracking global sports events and qualifiers.
- /world-cup: Interactive ESRI/GIS mapping of the 2026 World Cup venues.

## Technology Stack
- Frontend: Next.js 16 (App Router)
- Backend: Strapi CMS
- Maps: ESRI ArcGIS API
- Languages: English (en), Arabic (ar)

## Purpose
To bridge the gap between technical GIS data and passionate sports journalism for the Arabic & MENA regions.
